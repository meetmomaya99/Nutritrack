Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
Short version: NutriTrack stores all your data locally on your device only. We collect nothing. We send nothing. There are no servers, no accounts, no tracking.

What data we collect

None. NutriTrack does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All data you enter — meal logs, recipes, goals, and profile information — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device.

How your data is stored

Your data lives in your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device. It is not encrypted at rest (standard localStorage), so you should not store sensitive medical information.

Clearing your browser data or uninstalling the app will delete all NutriTrack data permanently.

Internet access

NutriTrack is a fully offline Progressive Web App (PWA). After the first load, it requires no internet connection to function. It does not make any network requests during normal use.

Third parties

NutriTrack does not use any third-party services, analytics, advertising networks, or tracking tools of any kind.

Children

NutriTrack does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be included in the next app update.

Contact

NutriTrack is a personal nutrition tracking tool. If you have questions, refer to the app's source code.
